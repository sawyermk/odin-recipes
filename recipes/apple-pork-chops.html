Apple Pork Chops

Apple pork chops cooking in cast iron pan

This is a flexible and straightforward recipe for pork chops with apple. Any pork meat will do if you accidently grab the wrong cut like I have before. Just make sure none of the pieces are too thick.

Ingredients

  • Pork chops
  • apple juice
  • apples
  • cooking oil
  • garlic
  • I gotta ask a friend

Steps

  1. Heat the pan to 400 degrees and add some cooking oil
  2. Sear the pork chops on high heat until nicely browned
  3. add cut up apples and apple juice to the pan and bring to a boil
  4. Reduce to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes
  5. Add the garlic and serve
