Lasagna

A finished tofu lasagna.

This is an easy and surprisingly tasty recipe that Pia Chesnais taught me in order to use up some extra tofu we had. I hope you enjoy and remember there are no rules.

Ingredients

  • no boil lasagna noodles
  • tomato sauce
  • ricotta chees
  • mozzarella cheese
  • garlic
  • olive oil
  • basil

Steps

  1. Fill the bottom of your baking dish with olive oil and tomato sauce or silken tofu
  2. Add a layer of no boil lasagna noodles
  3. Repeat the layering process alternating between tomato sauce and tofu/riccotta until the dish is nearly full. Add garlic as you like
  4. add a generous layer of mozzarella cheese to the top and basil if you have it
  5. bake uncovered for 30-45 min at 400
